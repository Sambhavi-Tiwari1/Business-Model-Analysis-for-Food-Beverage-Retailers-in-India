# Business Model Analysis for Food & Beverage Retailers in India

## 📊 Project Overview
**Duration:** August 2025 – October 2025  
**Type:** Self Project  
**Category:** Business Strategy & Market Analysis

### 🎯 Objective
Conduct a comprehensive analysis of the Indian food and beverage retail market to develop a robust business model framework and provide strategic recommendations for sustainable growth.

### 🔍 Key Areas of Analysis
- **Consumer Trends**: Analyzing changing preferences and purchasing behavior
- **Competitive Dynamics**: Evaluating market positioning and competitive advantages
- **Revenue Models**: Multi-channel revenue streams and pricing strategies
- **Cost Structures**: Supply chain optimization and operational efficiency
- **Market Opportunities**: High-potential product categories and segments

### 📈 Key Deliverables
1. **Market Analysis Report** - Comprehensive assessment of Indian F&B retail landscape
2. **Business Model Framework** - Structured approach to revenue generation and operations
3. **Consumer Insights Dashboard** - Visualization of preferences and buying patterns
4. **Strategic Recommendations** - Actionable insights for business growth and sustainability

### 🛠️ Technologies Used
- **Data Analysis**: Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- **Visualization**: Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly
- **Documentation**: Markdown, Jupyter Notebooks
- **Version Control**: Git & GitHub
